Superior Labels

About Us

The name "Superior" originates from the first letter of our founder’s name, Seren, and represents its literal meaning—a company that strives for excellence, setting high standards in commerce, services, and innovation. However, while we are labeled as Superior, we remain humble, always remembering that no matter how high we soar, we must keep our feet grounded.

At Superior Labels, we provide exceptional trade, services, and opportunities, ensuring that our growth also uplifts the community. We believe in breaking norms, creating opportunities, and supporting others in their ambitions.

Sub-Labels

R&B Law Authorities

R&B Law Authorities – The official legal team of Superior Labels. A functioning law firm co-owned by Rice and Brawly, providing legal services both in-lore and in practical gameplay situations.

Hiraya Labs

Hiraya Labs – An unreleased sub-label dedicated to innovation and automation. Specializes in selling auto-farm goods, helping streamline community farming operations with reliable, efficient tools.

Seren Sari-Sari Store

Seren Sari-Sari Store – A versatile general store owned by Superior Labels, offering a wide range of high-quality kits and assorted goods. Inspired by the Filipino sari-sari concept, it’s your one-stop shop for everything essential and unexpected.

Fishy Business

A reimagination of Rose’s first ever shop on her first Minecraft SMP more than 3 years ago. This new shop sells all kinds of items gathered from the ocean, with enchanted tridents being the main focus. The shop is located in Zone 3 - Bite and Barter of Genesis: The Experience.

Ro-Se

RO-SE is Superior Label’s monthly magazine, showcasing the standout members, groups, and projects of the Genesis community. Created and managed entirely by Seren and Rose, its name blends “Ro” and “Se” — a symbol of our teamwork. Each issue delivers high-quality visuals and stories that capture the spirit of our realm.

Core Values

Pioneering - We challenge norms, take bold risks, and redefine what's possible in the Minecraft realm business.

Synergy - We actively seek opportunities to collaborate with like-minded companies and businesses, creating greater value through combined efforts and shared goals.

Serendipity - Embracing unexpected opportunities, turning chance encounters into meaningful success.

Altruism - We believe in supporting and uplifting the community by providing opportunities, financial aid, and sponsorships to those in need.

Superiority - We strive for excellence in everything we do, setting high standards in business, governance, and innovation.
